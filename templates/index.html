About The Organization

Bloom foundation is a community organization based in the City of the eastern highlands, Guwahati. We set our eyes for the creation of a better future for the needful young buds through means of education and literacy development and securing a safe growing environment for them.

We believe that educational, literary and cultural engagement can ensure the broadening of outlooks and collective growth of all. Education and awareness must not be limited to only the select sections of our dwellings. All of us alike have the right to equal opportunities to identify and live up to our potential. This is what we work for, to provide a healthy and equitable environment of growth and development of the little blooming buds of the neglected halves of our society; involving them in as many engaging activities as we can and bringing a smile on their faces. Only by reducing the gulf between the privileged and unprivileged sections of the community will we be able to reach our goal of the betterment of all children.
